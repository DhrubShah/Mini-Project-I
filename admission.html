Admission Procedure

 If a student wants to be admitted at The Habile Students Home,he should fill in the application form and submit with the authentic sign of the parents.

 Along with the application form,the student should also submit the following documents.

  1. Pass Port Size Photo-2 Pics.
  2. Xerox of citizenship card.
  3. Xerox of college ID Card,if possible.

 After paying the allocated amount,the student can stay in the hostel as per necessity.

[Note:The presence of parents /Guardains or thier consent is a must for admission in the hostel.]


  Things to be brought after being admitted in the hostel: We provide low bed with dasna and a locker to every student.Except these,the students should bring other things by themselves.They are:

  1. Bed sheet.
  2. Pillow & Pillow Cover.
  3. Quilt(Sirak)/Blanket,thick in the winter and thin in the summer.
  4. Dress,as per necessity.
  5. Other stuffs as per personal requirement.
